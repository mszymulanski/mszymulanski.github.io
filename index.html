Cantonese Steamed Fish

  • 1 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 18 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 4 tilapia file
  • 1 scallion/green onion (julienned)
  • 2 bunches of cilantro
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 2 tbsp ginger (julienned)
  • 1 scallion/green onion (julienned)

Steps

  1. Combine the soy sauce, salt, sugar, and water into a small bowl and mix well.
  2. Prepare your steamer by heating it up.
  3. Place the fish on a plate into the steamer and cover it up for 10 minutes. You can check it for doneness by using a butter knife. If it easily cuts through to the bottom of the plate, your fish is done! Remove the fish from the steamer.
  4. Place the fish on a clean plate. Spread the cilantro and â…“ of the scallions on the fish.
  5. In a small saucepan on medium to high heat up the canola oil.
  6. Add ginger to the hot oil and brown it in about 1 minute.
  7. Add the rest of the scallions to the saucepan and let sizzle for less than a minute.
  8. Add the mixture from a bowl to the saucepan and keep it on high heat for about 30 seconds.
  9. Spoon the entire hot mixture over the fish and server immediately.
